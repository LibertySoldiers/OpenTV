# OPENTV V1.1.1

## CONTROL CENTER (NEW)
- **Independent Window**: New dedicated "Control Center" for settings, accessible via the gear icon or `S`.
- **Frameless Harmonization**: Consistent custom title bars across all windows with drag-region and integrated controls.
- **Real-time Toggles**: Cinema Light Engine and Glass Theme now activate instantly without needing a window reload.

## SECURITY & INTELLIGENCE
- **SHA-256 Integrity Check**: Automated cryptographic verification for updates to prevent tampering or corruption.
- **Playback Intelligence**: Added manual toggles for "Ultra-Low Latency" (HLS/DASH) and "Aggressive Buffering" for unstable links.
- **Parent-Child Logic**: Settings window is now linked to the main player, ensuring a cohesive focus and experience.

# OPENTV V1.1.0

## SECURITY & PRIVACY
- **Zero-Trust Sandbox**: Fully isolated rendering process.
- **RAM-Only Session**: Volatile storage for cookies and video cache (cleared on exit).
- **Hardened Lock**: Auto-purge of GPU/Shader cache on startup.
- **Lockdown Mode**: No external navigation or intrusive popups allowed. No server-side tracking.

## PERFORMANCE & UX
- **Context-Aware Ambient Light**: Blur engine now fades to black and pauses when app focus is lost to save resources.
- **Zero-Lag Engine**: Progressive list rendering and logo lazy-loading.

## PERSISTENCE
- **Hybrid Storage**: Favorites and Last Played Channel are securely persisted to local JSON, avoiding the volatile session.
